About ToolHub

ToolHub was created to provide simple, fast, and completely free online tools for everyday use. Many websites require registration, collect user data, or overload users with advertisements. ToolHub aims to offer a cleaner and privacy-focused alternative.

All tools available on this website work directly in your browser. No data is stored on our servers, and we do not require any account creation.

Whether you are a student calculating BMI, a writer counting words, a developer generating secure passwords, or someone splitting a restaurant bill — ToolHub provides quick solutions without complexity.

Our goal is to continuously expand this collection with more useful tools while maintaining performance and simplicity.
